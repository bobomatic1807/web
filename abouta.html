About

"Where the spirit does not work with the hand, there is no art." ~ Leonardo da Vinci

Robert is a Texas-born oil painter whose evocative landscapes capture the sublime beauty and vastness of the American landscape and beyond. Drawing inspiration from historical landscape masters like Sanford Gifford and Frederic Edwin Church, their work blends a reverence for nature’s grandeur with a distinctly personal, emotive touch. Robert brings a unique perspective shaped by years of experience in large-format photography, which focuses on meticulous attention to composition, light, and texture.

Each painting reflects a deep connection to the land, imbued with the warm hues and rugged spirit of America, while echoing the timeless drama of their artistic forebears. Robert’s paintings invite viewers to pause and immerse themselves in the quiet majesty of the natural world.
