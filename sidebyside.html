Lands End | Big Sur California


I was scrambling on the rocky coastline, scouting areas near Garapata State Park. It was a beautiful evening with moderate swells. As I climbed a pile of rocks, the view opened up, and it was obvious. As if calling, "take my picture"! I was scrambling to set up my wooden camera, just hoping the scene would hang on a few more minutes. Twenty years later, the painting captured exactly what I was feeling... grateful
